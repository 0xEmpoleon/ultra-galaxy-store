## What JOY Is

JOY positions itself as **the first GameFi hardware publisher** — a physical distribution gateway for Web3 games. The company sits at the intersection of gaming hardware, blockchain infrastructure, and play-to-earn economics. JOY is building both the hardware (a gaming console) and the ecosystem (token, game studio, developer partnerships) to bring on-chain gaming into a console form factor.

---

## Flagship Product: JOY Genesis

The **JOY Genesis** is the brand's flagship product — an AAA-grade gaming console purpose-built for Web3 gaming.

### Key Features

- **Web3 Gaming OS & JOY Store:** An open-source, Linux-based operating system built on Bazzite (https://bazzite.gg/). It features the **JOY Store** — a rebranded Hyperplay overlay acting as the core software layer to run on-chain games natively.
- **Integrated Hardware Wallet:** Built-in secure wallet for managing crypto assets, NFTs, and in-game tokens directly from the console
- **AAA On-Chain Games & Broad Compatibility:** Designed to run high-fidelity blockchain-native games seamlessly across nearly every EVM-compatible chain via the Hyperplay integration.
- **Steam & Epic Game Library Support:** Access traditional PC game libraries through the JOY Store, bridging Web2 and Web3 gaming
- **Play & Earn with $JOY Token:** Players earn JOY tokens through gameplay activity
- **Social & Integrated Security:** Hardware-level wallet security combined with social features

### Console Positioning

JOY Genesis is positioned as a **multichain AAA gaming console** — the physical distribution gateway that brings Web3 games out of browsers and into living rooms. While JOY was originally incubated within the Berachain ecosystem, the console is chain-agnostic by design, supporting a wide range of blockchain networks to maximise reach and compatibility.

### Pre-Order / Whitelist

JOY has run pre-booking campaigns and whitelist processes for the Genesis console. Whitelist holders receive benefits including boosted JOY ecosystem incentives, early access to leaks/renders, and giveaway eligibility.

## Ecosystem & Blockchain

### Multichain Strategy

JOY is a **multichain project**. While JOY was originally incubated by the **Berachain** ecosystem, it is not Berachain-exclusive and is not positioning Berachain as a primary focus for its upcoming launch. The project has evolved into a chain-agnostic platform that prioritises reach, performance, and strong ecosystem partnerships.

#### Chain Relationships (by tier)

**Grant Partners (deepest integration — primary emphasis):**
- **Somnia Network** — JOY has received a grant from Somnia Network; Somnia is a priority chain for the upcoming launch and should be highlighted prominently in chain-related messaging
- **Moonbeam** — JOY has received a grant from Moonbeam; this relationship reflects a meaningful level of ecosystem endorsement and should be emphasised alongside Somnia

**Active Chain Partners (close collaboration, no grant):**
- **Solana** — Working closely together; significant chain for the JOY ecosystem
- **BNB Chain** — Active collaboration and support
- **Incentiv** — Active support and collaboration

**Broad EVM Coverage & Software Layer:**
- **Hyperplay** — Hyperplay forms the core software layer of the JOY Genesis with its overlay rebranded as the **JOY Store** topping our Bazzite-based Linux OS. This partnership provides access to their vast game library, ecosystem partners, and engineering talent (including Heroic launcher devs and ex-ConsenSys devs). Through Hyperplay, JOY supports nearly every EVM-compatible chain, granting massive breadth without direct chain relationships.

**Historical / Legacy:**
- **Berachain** — JOY's incubation origin. The relationship is acknowledged but Berachain's underperformance means it is no longer a focal point for upcoming launch activities or chain messaging. Avoid leading with Berachain in forward-looking content.

### $JOY Token

- Native token of the JOY ecosystem
- Intended for play-to-earn rewards, in-game economies, and ecosystem incentives
- Players earn $JOY through gaming activity on the console
- Token economics designed to benefit both gamers and game developers/publishers

## Competitive Landscape & Differentiation

JOY occupies a unique niche — there are very few (if any) dedicated Web3 gaming consoles on the market. Competitors in adjacent spaces include:

- **Traditional consoles** (PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch) — no Web3 integration
- **Handheld PC devices** (Steam Deck, ROG Ally) — no native blockchain features
- **Web3 gaming platforms** (browser-based) — no dedicated hardware

JOY's differentiation is the **convergence of dedicated gaming hardware with native blockchain infrastructure** — a console that treats Web3 as a first-class citizen rather than an afterthought.
